Help Using sIRC

sIRC is all about giving you the user the best experience, while chatting away on various irc channels.

Some tips to make your use of sIRC more effective and smooth.

  • Use the <CTRL>+<Q> to quit sIRC
  • Use the <ALT>+<1 - 9> Key's to quickly toggle to between tabs

For help, questions and assistance you can always find us on irc.quakenet.org #sirchelp
